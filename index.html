Hopkins and the Blockchain

HopBlocks presents a collection of 2,500 unique Hopkins oriented designs with different rarities and benefits. HopBlocks are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Avalanche Blockchain. Our goal is to build a Hopkins blockchain community and provide people new exclusive benefits.

Our Roadmap

Hopkins Community

Welcome to the first Hopkins Blockchain community. We are building a new Decentralized Autonomous Organization in Web3.0 with exclusive benefits such as meeting alumni, access to social events, and many more. With NFT's and Web3.0 projects rising in popularity, we want a motivated community to help us push Web3.0 technologies limits.

Holders Involvement

We believe that our supporters of our project should be the priority. Therefore, we will give them to right to vote on the next advances for our project.

Future

We are planning on integrating our NFTs within the school by creating tokens that can be distributed for honors such as graduation certificates, deans list, academic all american, clubs, etc. We also want to create a digital currency ("JayCoin"), that will be sustainable and peer reviewed. This currency will be directed towards the use of university students to serve as a more flexible form of electronic campus currency.

Blockchain Education

One of the main purposes of this community is to education people on how to create Web3.0 DAOs as the crypto-world has been evolving quickly. In the past decade, people have finally grasped the concept of the internet and its use. Now we want to be on the forefront of educating the world about the new Web3.0!

Benefits

Being apart of a strong community led by Hopkins students, you will have the opportunity to gain access from networking alumni events, and lifetime membership to social events.

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for 'non-fungible token.' An NFT is basically a unique cryptographic token that exists on a blockchain and cannot be replicated. NFTs can be used to represent real-world items like artwork, memberships and more.
